TRIPS

For those seeking the ultimate white water challenge, our Extreme Rapids trip is the answer. With an advanced difficulty level and a 6-hour duration, this adrenaline-pumping experience departs from the rugged Mountain Base. Navigate through thrilling rapids and scenic landscapes with a maximum of 8 participants. Our experienced guides will lead the way, ensuring both safety and excitement. The package includes rafts, paddles, helmets, and a hearty lunch to recharge during your journey. Don't miss the opportunity to conquer the most intense rapids in this unforgettable adventure.

Embark on an exhilarating adventure through intermediate-level rapids. Departing from the scenic River Junction, this 4-hour trip is perfect for thrill-seekers seeking a balance between excitement and enjoyment. Our expert guides will accompany you on this memorable journey, ensuring safety and an unforgettable white water rafting experience. With a maximum of 10 participants, it's an ideal choice for small groups or individuals looking to make new friends along the way. Included in the trip are rafts, paddles, and a refreshing snack to keep you energized.

Perfect for families and beginners, the Family Fun Float is a 3-hour rafting trip departing from the serene Valley Station. Enjoy a leisurely float down the river, taking in the picturesque surroundings and bonding with your loved ones. With a beginner difficulty level and a maximum of 12 participants, this trip is designed for a relaxed and enjoyable experience. Our guides are there to assist and ensure everyone has a great time. The package includes rafts, paddles, life jackets, and a delightful snack. Make memories together in this family-friendly white water rafting adventure.
