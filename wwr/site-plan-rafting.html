Overview

Purpose

To establish a dynamic online presence for our rafting business that effectively showcases our services, engages our target audience, and facilitates seamless customer interactions while enhancing our brand visibility and credibility.

Audience

Our primary target audience for the water rafting website consists of adventure-seeking individuals and outdoor enthusiasts, typically in the age range of 18 to 45, who are physically active, value nature and excitement, and have a moderate to high disposable income. They have a keen interest in water sports, nature exploration, and adrenaline-pumping experiences. They want to embark on thrilling white-water rafting adventures in picturesque natural settings.

Who are the target customers, and what do they want?

Our target customers are adventure enthusiasts aged 18 to 45 who seek thrilling white-water rafting experiences. They want to challenge themselves, connect with nature, and create lasting memories through exhilarating outdoor adventures.

What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by accessing the website?

When accessing the website, they expect to find comprehensive information about our rafting trips, including itineraries, safety measures, and availability. They also anticipate finding customer testimonials and stories to gain insights into the quality of our services and a commitment to environmental responsibility.

How will my audience usually access my site (desktop, laptop, mobile device)?

Our audience typically accesses the site using both desktop and mobile devices. We've optimized our website to ensure a seamless and user-friendly experience on various platforms, ensuring that potential customers can access information and book their adventures with ease, whether they are at home or on the go.
